Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08/02/2025

At XrAId, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our app. By using XrAId, you agree to the terms outlined below. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use the app, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Email Address and Password: Collected via Firebase Authentication to securely create and manage your account.
  • Name, Birthdate, Blood Group, Weight, and Height: Stored securely in Firestore, which users can update later in their profile section.
  • X-Ray Image Data: X-ray images uploaded by users are stored in Firebase Storage, with the download URLs saved in Firestore for history tracking.

1.2 Images and Files

  • Camera Access: The app allows users to take pictures of X-ray scans. These images are sent to a server for processing.
  • Gallery Access: Users can upload X-ray images from their deviceâ€™s gallery, which are then sent to the server for processing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:
  • Account Authentication and Profile Management:Your email, name, birthdate, blood group, weight, and height are stored using Firebase Authentication and Firestore to securely manage your account.
  • History Tracking: Your past X-ray analyses are stored in Firestore for easy reference.
  • Image Analysis & AI Processing: Uploaded images are sent to a server hosted on Google Cloud Run, where AI models (YOLO and CNN) analyze the images for injury detection and projection classification. The processed image is then stored in Firebase Storage, and the download URL, along with the model results, is returned to the app. These results are displayed in the app and saved in Firestore for future reference.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:
  • Firebase: Used for authentication and securely storing your personal information, such as your email, name, birthdate, blood group, weight, and height.
  • Firestore: Used to store profile information and history of X-ray analyses.
  • Firebase Storage: Used to store X-ray images and their download URLs.
  • Google Cloud Run: Used to host the server where images are processed using AI models. Only X-ray images are sent to the server for analysis; no additional personal data is shared.

4. Permissions Requested by the App

To provide the core functionality of the app, we request the following permissions:
  • Camera Access: Allows users to take pictures of X-rays for analysis.
  • Gallery Access: Allows users to upload images from their device for AI processing.
  • Internet Access: Required for communication with Firebase, Firestore, Firebase Storage, and the Google Cloud Run server for processing X-rays.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal data, including profile information and X-ray analysis history, for as long as your account is active. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at aadithrp@gmail.com.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data using Firebase's security infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. User Control Over Data

You can control your personal data in the following ways:
  • Account Deletion: You can request to delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Profile Information: You can update or delete your profile information, such as your name and birthdate, directly within the app.

If we discover that a user under the age of 13 has created an account, we will promptly delete their data. Please contact us if you believe we have collected personal information from a user under 13.
